Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical design project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple designs like logos may take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive branding projects can take 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines in our project proposals.

Do you offer printing services only?

Yes, we offer standalone printing services for clients who already have their designs ready. We also provide design services if you need assistance creating your materials.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We prefer print-ready PDF files with bleeds and crops. We also accept AI, PSD, and INDD files. For best results, ensure all fonts are outlined and images are high-resolution (300 DPI).

Do you offer rush services?

Yes, we offer expedited services for an additional fee. Rush service availability depends on our current workload. Contact us to discuss your timeline requirements.

What is your revision policy?

We include 2-3 rounds of revisions in our design packages. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an hourly rate. We work closely with you to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. For design services, location is not a barrier. For printing, we can ship internationally, though shipping costs and times will vary.
