Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can you start my project?

We can typically start within 1-3 business days after project confirmation and deposit. Rush projects may be accommodated depending on our current workload.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary 30-minute consultations to discuss your project requirements, goals, and provide initial recommendations.

What is your payment process?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Communication is primarily through email, video calls, and project management tools.

Can I see examples of similar projects?

Yes, we'll share relevant portfolio pieces during our consultation that match your industry and project requirements.

What file formats do you deliver?

We provide all final files in the required formats for both digital and print use, including source files when applicable.
